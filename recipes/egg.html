Spiced Poached Spaghetti

Description:

Well, its got 470g of chocolate, 5 chicken thighs, 8oz of kidney beans, 264ml of red wine, and 310g of spaghetti in it.. what more do you need to know?!

Ingredients:

  • 470g of chocolate
  • 5 chicken thighs
  • 8oz of kidney beans
  • 264ml of red wine
  • 310g of spaghetti

Steps:

  1. Season the chocolate
  2. Cut the chicken thighs
  3. Season the kidney beans
  4. Whisk the red wine
  5. Put the spaghetti in the saucepan
  6. Weigh the kidney beans
  7. Peel the chicken thighs
  8. Shake the red wine
  9. Quarter the kidney beans
  10. Prepare the chicken thighs
Yum.
